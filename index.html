About Us

Dost is a Student-Led organization established in 2023 by three high school juniors at the North Carolina School of Science and Math. The organization aims to contribute to the education of homeschooled students by allowing them to excel in different fields with various content and support the material needs of the instructor.

How We Work!

We use different systems to support the educational understanding of the student and increase there ability to apply the topic.

Webinars for virtual interactive lectures

Our organization does virtual webinars in classrooms to support the learning of the student in an interactive platform that enables them to socialize.

Lecture videos made specificly for student needs

Each student is assigned specific videos aiming to support the understanding of the topic. Each video is prepared according to the state educational guidelines.
